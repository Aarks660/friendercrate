The Friendercrate Demonlist is the most popular Demonlist ranking of Gotmerty Dahs's (not) hardest demons with trivigintillion of visitors each day :D
The Demonlist is divided into 3 categories: the Main List, Extended List and the Legacy List. The Main List shows the current top 5 hardest completions. The Extended List shows the completions after the top 5th completion uptil the top 15th completion. The Legacy List shows the completions which were previously on the Main or Extended List and got moved out of the top 15, sad :(
